CEO

Neal Sahni

With over 32 years in commodity trading, Neal Sahni began his career in 1991 with R.Veljee & Sons in India. He later moved to Singapore, holding senior roles at Concordia Agri Trading Pte Ltd and Midstar Singapore Pte Ltd. In 2017, he founded the Mera Group in London, serving as CEO.

Chairman

Omar Saleh Babaker

Omar Babaker brings over 36 years of experience to the soft commodity industry. Abu Kass stands out as a renowned rice brand across the Middle East. Moreover, Babaker Group is involved in various sectors such as construction, real estate, and operates a supermarket chain named Babaker in Riyadh.

CFO

Nazeer Datoobhoy

As a Chartered Accountant with nearly 30 years of experience, he has contributed to renowned firms including Arthur Andersen, Pricewaterhouse Coopers, Morgan Stanley, and various startup funds, holding significant senior global positions.

Director of Trading

Allan Perk

With nearly two decades of engagement in the commodity sector, Allan has served in diverse capacities including Trader, Derivatives Trader, Portfolio Manager, Adviser, and Head Trader. His experience spans esteemed firms such as Noble Group, Edesia, Louis Dreyfus, and Tradefield.
